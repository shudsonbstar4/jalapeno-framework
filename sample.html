Jalapeno.css -- theme-able skins for your web app

The idea for Jalapeno.css came from the need to quickly and easily change CSS stylesheets based on context. Whether accessing through desktop or mobile, cutting edge or minimal, animated or accessible, Jalapeno.css offers the options to change your skins based on your user's current needs.

List of components:

  • Typography
  • Buttons
  • Forms
  • Labels
  • Layouts
  • HTML Elements
  • Color Schemes
  • Accessibility
